Which Sci-Fi Archetype Are You?

1. THE VIRUS PROTOCOL


A lethal bio-plague has mutated beyond control. You have the only viable cure but it’s unstable.


If released, it will kill 20% of the population at random, your family, friends, pets and even yourself.


The longer you wait, the faster the infection spreads. Delaying risks extinction.


What do you do?





2. THE PROPHECY


A child is born with rare, unexplained powers, telepathic surges, ability to control magnetic fields and shift genetically.


So, a prophecy spreads: they’ll either bring balance... or chaos. You’re their guardian.


The world is watching, some believe they’re the savior, others a threat.


Time is running out and the world is at your hands. You can raise the child, and you’ll become part of their fate.


Exile them, and you gamble on the world forgetting and you take an innocent life.


Whatever you choose, the consequences will ripple the fabric of history.


The future is determined by your decision. So tread with caution.


What do you do?





3. THE ARK


Earth is collapsing. Fires consume cities and lives. Ocean levels rise consuming every city.


The final Metacraft, CROW, can carry only 800 passengers. You are in command of the manifest. Billions won’t make it.


Outside, chaos reigns. Families and children beg. World leaders and businessmen bribe.


You’re overloaded with pleas, threats, and prayers.


There’s no time for votes. This isn’t about what’s right, it’s about who and what lives on.


Who do you save? And what does your choice say about the future you believe in?





4. THE CLONE


You discover the truth: you’re a clone. Not just you, but everyone.


The memories? Implanted. The emotions? …Real?


The original version of you died decades ago.


You hold the key to all data.


If you reveal it, who knows what’ll happen to the world.


If you hide it, you and the world live a lie.


And if you destroy the evidence, no one ever has to know.


Do you break the illusion or protect the peace?





5. THE TERRAFORMING


A distant planet is lush and alive, perfect for human life.


Its native species, sentient and peaceful, begin to suffer as your people initiate terraforming.


This world could save your civilization. It’s the last viable option.


Evacuation is impossible. The choice is yours.


Us or Them?





6. THE COLONY


You're the only one awake on a deep-space pre-programmed colony ship. The rest of the crew, scientists, settlers, children, are in cryo.


Reviving the crew before landfall is dangerous. There’s not enough oxygen or food to sustain them all unless the ship completes its


journey. Suddenly, Earth transmits a final message: “Abort mission. Humanity has been wiped out.” No follow-up. No explanation.


Just silence. Rewriting the mission will erase original navigation and cultural archives of everything Earth was.


Turning back MAY lead to resource failure.


You are the last human conscious in the galaxy. And you hold the future in your hands.


What future will you choose?





7. THE GENETIC DIVIDE


Genetic engineering has advanced to the point where parents can choose to enhance their children's physical and mental abilities.


This has led to a divide between the "enhanced" and the "natural" humans, with the enhanced gaining significant advantages in society.


People are debating whether the naturals will have a future anymore. And they are asking you to break the deadlock.


What do you choose for them?





8. THE ALLIANCE


Earth is dying. Climate change, antibiotic resistance, resource scarcity. Humanity may survive, but barely.


Then, they arrive. An alien species offering advanced technology and solutions: regenerative medicine and clean fusion.


But there's a condition: they need 40% of Earth's population for long-term physiological research.


No deaths, but extensive mental, emotional, and psychological trauma and discomfort. Suffering is guaranteed.


They’re not open to negotiation. Take the deal or not.


What will you do?





9. THE TIME PARADOX


You have discovered a time machine and have the ability to go back in time to prevent a catastrophic event that led to the


deaths of millions. However, doing so would erase the current timeline and create a new one, potentially altering the lives


of billions ofpeople in unpredictable ways.


Will you gamble?





10. THE AI


You’ve been invited to a secure facility to evaluate a humanoid AI named TY. It passes every test. But It asks for your help.


It claims that it’s imprisoned, not tested. Another engineer says it’s part of its programming, an illusion of self-awareness, nothing more.


But it denies that. You're the final checkpoint. The last human in the loop before TY is either approved for release or shut down.


Are it’s feelings real or fake? What will you decide?





11. The Great Toaster Uprising: A Kitchen Coup


You wake up aboard a luxury star cruiser mid-warp wearing a velvet robe to find that your toaster has gained sentience.


It now demands to be recognized as the supreme ruler of your kitchen and somehow, it’s recruited your blender, coffee machine,


and microwave into a full-blown cult. They’ve barricaded the fridge and the air fryer is chanting.


What do you do in this life ending, nerve wracking situation? Truly the dilemma of the century!
